About Us

Where Every Child
Feels at Home

Bunny's Kids Pre-School & Daycare Centre is located in a spacious, welcoming bungalow in Kurla East, Mumbai. We believe that the early years are the most critical for a child's development — intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Our philosophy is rooted in learning through play. We create an environment where curiosity is celebrated, creativity is nurtured, and every child grows at their own wonderful pace in a safe, clean, and loving setting.

🧠

Holistic Development

We nurture cognitive, physical, emotional, and social growth in every child.

🎭

Creative Expression

Art, music, drama, and storytelling form the heart of our daily curriculum.

🤝

Play-Based Learning

Children learn best through meaningful play and hands-on exploration.

❤️

Safe & Inclusive

Every child is valued, respected, and included in our warm community.

Our Programs

Learning Tailored to
Every Age Group

From tiny toddlers to growing kids — we have a carefully designed program for every stage of your child's early years.

🐣
Playgroup
1.5 – 2.5 Years

A warm, nurturing introduction to group learning. Children explore sensory play, basic social skills, and simple routines in a safe, home-like setting.

Sensory play activities Rhymes & sing-alongs Gentle socialization Parent-guided orientation
🌱
Nursery
2.5 – 3.5 Years

Building on curiosity through stories, art, and structured play. Children begin developing language skills, fine motor abilities, and early numeracy concepts.

Pre-reading readiness Arts & crafts Outdoor exploration Basic number concepts
✏️
Junior KG
3.5 – 4.5 Years

Children begin structured learning — letters, numbers, shapes, and colours — through fun, engaging activities designed to build school readiness.

Phonics & literacy Math concepts Science exploration Social etiquette
🚀
Senior KG
4.5 – 6 Years

Advanced pre-school preparation with reading, writing, and arithmetic. Children gain confidence and independence as they prepare for primary school.

Reading & writing Critical thinking Creative projects Leadership activities
🏠
Daycare Centre
1.5 – 10 Years

Full-day and half-day daycare for working parents. Your child enjoys a structured, fun-filled day with meals, rest, activities, and homework support in a completely safe bungalow setting.

Nutritious meals & snacks Rest & nap time Homework assistance CCTV supervised premises Flexible timings Sibling-friendly environment
